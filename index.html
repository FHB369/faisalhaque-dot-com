I am a third year PhD student at Syracuse University iSchool with a focus on the security and performance of distributed systems, blockchain, and cloud computing. I received my bachelor’s degree (2020) in Computer Science and Engineering from the Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST), Bangladesh. My research focuses on improving blockchain performance and security by addressing conflicting transactions and detecting malicious activities. I use parallelism, dependency management, and multi-agent reinforcement learning to enhance efficiency, resilience, and security in decentralized networks.
